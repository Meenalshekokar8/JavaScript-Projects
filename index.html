JavaScript Projects Basic to Advance

I started my JavaScript journey in July 2024, and since then, I’ve completed over 30 projects. This has been an exciting and rewarding experience, as I've been able to apply new concepts quickly and gain hands-on experience with JavaScript. Each project presented different challenges, helping me solidify my understanding of core principles like variables, loops, functions, and objects. The practical learning from these projects has helped me bridge the gap between theory and real-world application.

These projects have ranged from small scripts to more complex web applications, giving me a well-rounded foundation in front-end development.

As I continue learning, I’m excited to explore even more advanced topics like JavaScript frameworks (React, Vue) and server-side development with Node.js. Completing over 30 projects in such a short time has been a great achievement for me, and I look forward to continuing to grow my skills and tackle even more complex challenges in the future.
